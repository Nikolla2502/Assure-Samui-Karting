Team

Assure Samui Karting is a karting structure based on the island of Koh Samui in the south of Thailand and engaged in the Rotax Max 2021 competition. With the help of our partners we will give the best of ourselves to represent the island of Koh Samui.
We can also provide you with assistance in competition, for training and preparation or adjustment of your equipment.

More informations about the team

The Competition

The Rotaxmax Thailand challenge is a competition of karts equipped with Rotaxmax engine delivering a power of about 30hp for a chassis of 80 kg.
It takes place for the season 2021 on the circuit of Bira near Pattaya.
